I love performing at colleges! It's literally my favorite type of crowd because they're young,fun and don't file FIRs. I have a lot of jokes about the education system, pop culture and social media that go well with this kind of crowd. And we've created a standup, music, sketch and improv mixed show called Backbenchers which completely revolves around college life.

Some of the colleges I've performed at include: ISB Hyderabad, BITS Pilani, BITS Hyderabad, BITS Goa, IIT Powai, Jai Hind College, IIM Trichy, NIT-K, JP University, NMIMS, St. Xavier's College, VIT, Sophia College, SIES College, KEM College, DY Patil Mumbai.
